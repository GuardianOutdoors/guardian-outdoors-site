Who We Are

Florida Based. Family Owned. Gas Focused.

Guardian Outdoors is a division of Guardian Gas Solutions LLC. A licensed propane and natural gas contractor serving North and Central Florida. Outdoor kitchens are our specialty.

Why We Exist

Outdoor Living, Done Right.

Florida is a backyard state. People want grills that work, fire features that turn on with a knob, and patio heaters that do not run out of propane during a cookout. We make that part, the gas part, disappear into the background.

We sell premium outdoor kitchens because we got tired of homeowners struggling to find one that was not a flat pack project from a big box store. We hook up the gas because that is what we have always done.
