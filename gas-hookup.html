Propane Or Natural Gas

Both. Whatever Your House Has.

Have city natural gas already? We tap it and run a permanent line to your backyard. Out in the country on propane? We size and connect to your existing tank, or set up a new one.

Either way, no more hauling 20-pound tanks during a cookout. One line. Sized correctly. Pressure tested. Ready to feed everything you need.

How It Goes

One Visit. One Line. Done Right.

Site Visit
We come out, walk the property, look at where the kitchen is going and where the gas needs to come from. No charge to look.
Quote and Schedule
Fixed scope quote with everything spelled out. Once you say go, we put you on the schedule.
Run It and Light It
We trench, run line, pressure test, leak check, and fire it up with you on site. You are cooking the same day.
